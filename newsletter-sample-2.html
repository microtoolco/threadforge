I Bootstrapped My Startup to $50K MRR in 8 Months. Here's My Playbook.

Eight months ago, I quit my job with $5,000 in savings and a half-baked idea. Today, my SaaS does $50K/month with zero outside funding. Here's exactly how I did it — no fluff, just actionable steps.

$0 Funding Raised
$50K Monthly Revenue
8 Months

The Foundation: Solving a Real Problem

I didn't start with a "startup idea." I started with my own frustration. Every day, I was spending 3 hours doing something that should take 30 minutes. That frustration became my product.

The key insight: Your best startup idea is probably something you're already annoyed about.

My Step-by-Step Playbook

  1. Week 1-2: Talk to 50 potential customers. Not surveys — real conversations. I used Twitter DMs and got a 40% response rate.
  2. Week 3-4: Build an embarrassingly simple MVP. Mine was literally a Python script with a UI slapped on top.
  3. Month 2: Launch on Product Hunt. Got #3 of the day. First 100 customers.
  4. Month 3-6: Double down on what works. For me, it was Twitter content + SEO.

Pro tip: Your first 100 customers don't come from ads. They come from being genuinely helpful in communities where your customers hang out.

What I'd Do Differently

Charge more from day one. I started at $9/month when I should have started at $29. Underpricing attracts the wrong customers and kills your motivation.
